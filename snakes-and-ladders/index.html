Snakes and ladders is a game where two or more players race to reach the finish.

On their turn, each player rolls the die. They then move forward by that number of squares. If they land on the head of a snake at the end of their turn then they slide down to the tail, and if they land at the foot of a ladder then they travel up that ladder.
